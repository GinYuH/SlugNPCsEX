Note: This is an unofficial port of the mod of the same name from 1.4.3 with given permission.

Slug Town Pets adds the scrapped Calamity town pet slugs designed by IbanPlay into the game!
Many thanks to IbanPlay for creating the design and sprites and giving permission to add them in.
** This does NOT require Calamity Mod **

How to obtain:
Albino - Defeat EoC.
Crypt - Defeat Deerclops.
Storm - Defeat Duke Fishron.
Devourer - Defeat Moon Lord.
Universe - Have Zenith in your inventory.
